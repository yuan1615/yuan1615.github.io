Biography

I received the B.S. degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Pingdingshan University, China, in 2015. I am currently pursuing the M.S. degree in Statistics with the School of Mathematics, Physics and Statistics, Shanghai University of Engineering Science, China.

My current research interests include:

  • high-dimension statistical inference
  • sparse statistical analysis
  • and their applications in financial risk management
  • machine learning
  • deep learning
